What is a GPA?

A GPA, or grade point average is a showings of the average grade you have scored in your classes. Your GPA is used to determine if you are up to the standards of your educators and future educators. There are two methods of calculating gpa, weighted and unweighted. Weighted allows for different classes to be worth more points towards the users final GPA and unweighted sets them all to be equal in value. This GPA calculator supports both.
